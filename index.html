About VolunteerHub

VolunteerHub is an Event Team Allocation and Management Platform designed to eliminate poor volunteer distribution during community events.

Instead of allowing one team to become overcrowded while other teams remain understaffed, VolunteerHub intelligently organizes volunteers into different task groups with predefined capacities.
